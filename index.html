✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NexChain and our ecosystem.

What makes NexChain different from other blockchains?

NexChain utilizes a revolutionary hybrid consensus mechanism combining PoS with DAG technology, achieving 100,000+ TPS while maintaining full decentralization and security. Our EVM compatibility ensures easy migration from existing chains.

How can I buy $NXC tokens?

$NXC tokens are available on major exchanges including Binance, Coinbase, and Uniswap. You can also purchase directly through our platform using credit card or bank transfer. Visit our dApp to get started.

What are the staking rewards?

Staking rewards currently range from 8-15% APY depending on the lock-up period. Longer staking periods receive higher rewards. Rewards are distributed daily and can be claimed or auto-compounded.

Is NexChain secure?

Security is our top priority. NexChain has been audited by CertiK, Quantstamp, and Trail of Bits. We maintain a $10M bug bounty program and employ continuous security monitoring with 24/7 incident response.

How do I become a validator?

To become a validator, you need to stake a minimum of 10,000 $NXC and run our validator node software. Full documentation and hardware requirements are available in our developer portal.

What wallets support NexChain?

NexChain is supported by MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Ledger, Trezor, and our native NexWallet. Any EVM-compatible wallet can connect to our network by adding our custom RPC endpoint.
